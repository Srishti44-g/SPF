Our Mission

Shreesurya Pushpa Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to uplifting underprivileged communities through sustainable development initiatives. Founded in 2010, our foundation works tirelessly to create opportunities for education, provide healthcare access, promote environmental sustainability, and empower women through skill development.

We believe in a holistic approach to community development, addressing immediate needs while building long-term solutions that enable communities to thrive independently. Our dedicated team of volunteers and professionals work hand-in-hand with local communities to understand their unique challenges and develop tailored solutions.

Like the sun (Surya) that brings light and energy to all life, we strive to bring positive change and illuminate the path toward a better future for those in need.

Our Programs

Education & Literacy

We provide quality education to underprivileged children, operate after-school learning centers, train teachers, and supply educational materials to resource-limited schools.

Healthcare Initiatives

Our mobile medical camps, health awareness programs, and nutrition support services ensure that essential healthcare reaches remote and underserved communities.

Environmental Sustainability

Through tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, and renewable energy projects, we promote environmental consciousness and sustainable living practices.

Women Empowerment

We conduct skill development workshops, vocational training, and microfinance initiatives to help women become financially independent and community leaders.

Food & Shelter Programs

We provide food security and shelter to the homeless and marginalized communities through our feeding programs and housing initiatives.

Skill Development

Our vocational training programs equip youth with essential skills, enabling them to secure employment and contribute to their communities.

Community Development

We work with local communities to identify their needs and implement sustainable development projects that enhance their quality of life.

Urban Development & Poverty Alleviation

We focus on improving living conditions in urban slums through infrastructure development, sanitation projects, and poverty alleviation programs.

Agriculture

We promote sustainable agricultural practices, provide training to farmers, and support organic farming initiatives to enhance food security.
